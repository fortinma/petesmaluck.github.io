Who we are

Roque Roofing is located in Hamilton, Ontario. Specializing in built-up roofing, we are also approved applicators of most major manufacturers of modified bitumen, cold applied roof restorations and single ply membranes. In addition to new construction and re-roofing, we provide preventative maintenance services. Customers are protected with Roque Roofing through excellent financial stability, complete insurance and bonding protection.

Roque Roofing provides quality contracting services and continues to exceed the expectations of owners and consultants.

Our History

With just a little over 10 years of experience in flat roofing, Manuel Roque, Owner, ventured out on his own and established ROQUE ROOFING in 1980. Now in its second generation, the company continues to be owned and operated by Manuel and his family.

In addition to Manuel’s 40 plus years of flat roofing experience, he has trained our crews how to install the best quality of roof systems and since Roque Roofing takes pride in our work we ensure all project requests are met to the customer’s satisfaction.

At Roque Roofing, our Health and Safety Policy is very simple, “SAFETY FIRST”. This fundamental policy ensures that no worker will adopt a procedure or undertake a work activity that is likely to cause injury to that person or any other workers.

Roque Roofing serves all of southwestern Ontario, providing professional friendly service and quality commercial roofing. Certified to install many types of roofing systems, quickly and efficiently, Roque Roofing has the expertise to get the job done!
